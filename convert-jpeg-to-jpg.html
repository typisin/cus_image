JPEG to JPG Converter FAQ

Is JPEG the same as JPG?

Yes. JPEG and JPG are the same format. The only difference is the file extension.

Can I turn a .jpeg into a .jpg without changing quality?

If you simply rename the extension, the file stays the same. If you re-encode, quality depends on the selected quality level.

Does this tool upload my image?

No. Conversion runs locally in your browser.
